Sophia Johnson

Sophia has been a driving force in the Dragon Boating scene, known for her endurance, leadership, and dedication to teamwork. With multiple championship wins and a passion for mentoring young athletes, she has truly earned her title as Rower of the Year.

Key Achievements

  • Gold Medal – National Dragon Boat Sprint (2024)
  • Captain of Silver Wave Team (2023–2025)
  • Founded “PaddleStrong” youth training initiative
  • Over 50+ race victories globally
