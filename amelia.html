Amelia Chen

Amelia combines grace and power on the water, inspiring teammates with her relentless commitment and passion. A master strategist and fierce competitor, Amelia’s technique and endurance make her one of the finest dragon boat rowers of her generation.

Key Achievements

  • Gold Medalist at Asia-Pacific Championships (2023)
  • Named “Best Female Rower” three years running
  • Lead paddler in the undefeated National Team (2022-2024)
  • Advocate for youth rowing development programs
