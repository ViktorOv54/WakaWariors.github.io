What is the Team Sprint?

The Team Sprint is one of the most exciting events in the Dragon Boating Festival. It features multiple teams racing over a short, intense distance that tests power, speed, and perfect synchronization.

Race Details:

  • Distance: 200 meters
  • Team Size: 12 paddlers + 1 drummer + 1 steerer
  • Time Limit: Approximately 1 minute
  • Focus: Acceleration, coordination, and timing
